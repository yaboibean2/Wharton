Total Insights AI Investing Research

An AI-powered investment analysis platform that synthesizes data from multiple financial APIs and language models to deliver comprehensive, multi-factor stock and portfolio research.

What the App Does

Total Insights AI Investing Research runs a panel of specialized AI agents — covering valuation, growth momentum, macroeconomic regime, risk, and sentiment — to produce a unified investment score and written analysis for any publicly traded stock. Key capabilities include:

  • Multi-agent analysis with customizable weighting
  • Real-time and historical market data from Polygon, Alpha Vantage, and Yahoo Finance
  • AI-generated research summaries via OpenAI and Perplexity
  • Portfolio-level batch analysis with parallel processing
  • One-click export to Google Docs and Google Sheets
  • Performance tracking with autonomous learning

How Your Data Is Used

The App processes only the stock tickers and analysis parameters you provide. It does not collect personal information, use cookies or trackers, or store your Google credentials on any external server. If you choose to export results to Google Docs or Sheets, the App requests only the minimum OAuth scopes required. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions.

For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.
